Conclusion

Research, communication, and technology are very important in every discipline, including the field of criminal justice. Research is necessary to learn and develop techniques, locate and ‘profile’ criminals, and it helps influence criminal justice policy. Communication skills are also very important in criminal justice. You need to have strong verbal, non-verbal, and written communication skills to be successful. Lastly, technology is crucial to the development of the criminal justice system because as technology advances and develops, so does the field of criminal justice. Criminal justice is a constantly changing and influential system that uses research, communication, and technology to run.
