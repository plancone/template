Blog #4

The Chrysler ad that we watched in class gives me chills every time it comes on. It encorperates images of Detroit, the song Lose Yourself by Eminem, and the slogan Imported From Detroit. The purpose of this ad is to show that yes the city of Detroit has gone through some difficult times, and no this city is not like any other big city in our country, but it does know luxury. It shows that Detroit knows hard work, and it is capable of making an amazing car. It says Imported From Detroit because unless you live in or around Detroit, you do not know anything about the city. You only assume what you have seen in the news paper is all you need to know about the city, and this ad proves that assumption wrong.
