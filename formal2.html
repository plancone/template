Introduction

When talking about research, people usually think of science based research that takes place in laboratories. However, this is not always the case. Actually, research takes places in every academic discipline, and criminal justice is one of these academic disciplines. Along with research, communication is commonly not thought of when you think of criminal justice, but it is a crucial point in the criminal justice system. Technology is another important component when discussing the criminal justice system. Criminal justice relies on technology far more than most people realize. The field of criminal justice is constantly using research, communication, and technology to shape and influence the criminal justice system. Although the criminal justice system can be complex it is still influenced by these aspects.
